## Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include:

- Your device’s Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address)  
- Device type and operating system  
- App usage data (e.g. screens visited, session duration)  
- Diagnostic and crash data  

This information is used to:
- Maintain and improve the Application  
- Ensure stable and reliable connection to your TV  
- Diagnose and fix technical issues  

---

## TV Connectivity

The Application connects to your Sony BRAVIA TV over your local Wi-Fi network.

- Communication between your device and TV happens **within your local network**  
- The Application does **not store or transmit your TV content externally**  
- Pairing information (e.g. authentication tokens/cookies) is stored securely on your device to maintain connection
