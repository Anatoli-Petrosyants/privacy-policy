# Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the **CtrlTV — Universal TV Remote** app (hereby referred to as "Application") for iOS devices, created by Anatoli Petrosyants (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Freemium service. This service is provided "AS IS".

---

## Information Collection and Use

The Application may collect technical information while you use it. This information may include:

- Your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address)
- Device type, model, and operating system version
- App usage data (e.g. screens visited, session duration, features used)
- Diagnostic and crash data
- Local Wi‑Fi network information necessary to discover and pair with your TV (e.g. local IP addresses of TVs found on the same network)

This information is used to:

- Maintain and improve the Application
- Ensure stable and reliable connection to your TV
- Diagnose and fix technical issues
- Detect abuse or fraud

---

## TV Connectivity

The Application connects to your TV over your local Wi‑Fi network. Supported brands currently include Sony BRAVIA, Samsung, LG, Roku, Hisense, TCL, Vizio, Panasonic, and Philips. Communication uses each manufacturer's local protocol (HTTP, WebSocket, or DIAL) entirely within your local network.

- Communication between your device and TV happens **within your local network only**.
- The Application does **not stream, store, or transmit your TV content externally**.
- Pairing material (authentication cookies, tokens, or PIN-derived credentials) is stored on your device to maintain the connection. Tokens are scoped per TV IP address and can be removed at any time by deleting the TV from the app or uninstalling the Application.

## Personal Data

The Application does **not require account creation** and does not actively collect personally identifiable information.

If you contact the Service Provider (e.g. via the in-app "Contact Us" email flow), your provided information (such as email address, device model, app version, and any diagnostic log you attach) will be used only to respond to your request.

---

## Diagnostic Logs

The Application maintains a local diagnostic log on your device for troubleshooting. The log records app actions, TV pairing/command events, and network errors. The log:

- Stays on your device by default.
- Is shared with the Service Provider **only** if you explicitly attach it when contacting support.
- May contain local IP addresses of TVs you've connected to, vendor names, and timestamped event descriptions. It does not contain TV content, screen captures, or audio.

You can clear the log at any time from the Settings screen.
